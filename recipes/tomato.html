Tomato Recipe

Description :

Tomatoes are the edible, often red, pulpy berries of the tomato plant, belonging to the nightshade family. They are botanically classified as fruits but are commonly used as vegetables in culinary applications. Tomatoes come in various colors, including red, yellow, orange, and even purple, and can be round, oval, or pear-shaped. They are known for their juicy flesh and slightly tangy taste, and are a versatile ingredient in salads, sauces, and many other dishes.

Ingredients :

  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Oil
  • Spices like turmeric, chili powder, and coriander

Steps :

  1. Heat oil in a Pan, add mustard seeds and crackle them.
  2. Then add curry leaves, chopped Onion and saute till translucent.
  3. Then add chopped tomatoes saute them for 5 minutes.
  4. Add sambar powder, salt and 1 small cup water mix it well and saute for a while.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
